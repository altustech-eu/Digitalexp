// 03 — PRODUCTION PROCESS
Idea to
Published Asset
Every piece of content follows the same 10-stage manufacturing process — with defined owners, SLAs, and quality gates at each transition.

Stage-by-Stage Production SOP

Each stage has a defined owner, input requirements, quality checklist, and handoff protocol. No stage begins without the prior stage being signed off.

01
Research & Trend Mining
Social listening analyst mines trending keywords (Germany jobs, Ausbildung salary), competitor content gaps, YouTube search demand, and comment sentiment. Outputs structured insight report.
Owner: Research Analyst SLA: Daily Output: Trend Report
02
Idea Generation & Scoring
Team generates ideas from research. Each idea is scored on: Demand Signal (40%), Emotional Trigger (30%), Production Complexity (20%), Brand Fit (10%). Ideas scoring >70 are approved for scripting.
Owner: Content Strategist SLA: 2x / week Output: Approved Idea Bank
03
Script Writing
Script writer uses the HOOK → BODY → CTA framework. Hook: First 3–5 seconds must stop scroll. Body: Structured narrative with proof points. CTA: Single clear action. AI suggests improvements before human approval.
Script Structure Template
HOOK (0–5s)"Did you know you can earn ₹70,000/month in Germany after 12th grade?" PROBLEM (5–20s)Paint the pain — no career options, uncertain future, parents worried SOLUTION (20–50s)Introduce Ausbildung pathway — structured, paid, visa-backed PROOF (50–80s)Success story, employer partnerships, placement numbers CTA (80–90s)"Book your free counseling today — link in bio"
Owner: Script Writer SLA: 10 scripts/day Output: Approved Script
04
Storyboard Creation
Creative Director builds scene-by-scene visual breakdown. Each scene specifies: shot type (wide/medium/close), camera angle, talent positioning, B-roll suggestions, text overlay placement, and transition type.
Owner: Creative Director SLA: 24 hrs post-script
05
Production Planning & Resource Booking
Local Production Coordinator books studio, confirms talent availability, tests equipment, prepares checklist. Daily batch shoots planned 48 hours in advance. Backup talent and equipment always identified.
Pre-Shoot Checklist
✓ Script printed & approved
✓ Talent confirmed & briefed
✓ Studio booked & accessible
✓ Camera — 4K ready
✓ 3-point lighting setup
✓ Lavalier mic tested (-60dB)
✓ Backup talent identified
✓ B-roll shot list prepared
06
Shoot Execution
A-roll (direct to camera, clear voice, natural delivery) + B-roll (supporting visuals, context scenes, environment shots). Multiple takes. Quality check before wrapping. Footage uploaded same day.
Owner: Cameraman + Producer Output: 15–20 reels/day
07
Video Editing
Fast-paced editing optimized for retention. No dead frames. Subtitles mandatory. Brand-compliant transitions. Music added. Auto-subtitles reviewed and corrected. Duration finalized for platform (60s Reels / 8–15 min YouTube).
Owner: Video Editor SLA: 10–15 edits/day
08
Thumbnail & Graphic Design
CTR-optimized thumbnail: bold text (3–5 words max), expressive face, high contrast, branded colors. A/B variant always created. Designer creates 20 thumbnails/day. AI suggests best-performing variant.
Owner: Graphic Designer Output: 20 designs/day
09
SEO Optimization
Title includes primary keyword in first 60 chars. Description front-loaded with searchable terms. Tags cover primary + secondary + LSI keywords. Chapters added for YouTube. All metadata reviewed before scheduling.
Owner: SEO Analyst SLA: 4 hrs post-edit
10
Publish, Distribute & Track
Scheduled via publishing tool. Caption + hashtags prepared. WhatsApp/Telegram community push. Performance tracking begins at T+1hr, T+24hr, T+7d. Low performers flagged for optimization within 48 hours.
Owner: Social Manager Output: Live + tracked KPI: CTR, Views, Leads

Key Role Profiles

👑
Head of Content (CCO)
Corporate · Governance Layer
Core Mandate

Owns content as a revenue engine. Final brand authority. Investment decisions.

Daily Responsibilities
  • Review content performance analytics (daily dashboard)
  • Approve content strategy and campaign themes
  • Govern brand narrative and messaging consistency
  • Content investment allocation and vendor decisions
Key Deliverables
  • Monthly content strategy report
  • Quarterly content investment review
  • Brand playbook (updated quarterly)
✍️
Script Writer
Creative Team · Execution Layer
Core Mandate

Converts ideas into compelling, conversion-optimized content scripts.

Daily Output Standard
  • 10 short-form scripts per day (Reels format)
  • 2–3 long-form scripts per week (YouTube)
  • Hook testing — minimum 2 variants per script
  • Submit all scripts by 12pm for same-day shoots
Key Deliverables
  • Script bank (version-controlled in KBM)
  • Hook testing report (weekly)
  • CTA effectiveness analysis (monthly)
📍
Local Production Coordinator
Production · Ground Operations
Core Mandate

Most critical role for scaling. Owns every shoot from booking to upload.

Daily Responsibilities
  • Confirm next-day shoot schedule by 6pm
  • Verify talent availability and studio booking
  • Equipment test before each shoot
  • Supervise shoot quality in real-time
  • Upload raw footage same day; assign editors
Key Deliverables
  • Daily shoot completion report
  • Footage upload confirmation log
  • Quality control sign-off sheet
📊
Content Data Analyst
Analytics · Intelligence Layer
Core Mandate

Converts performance data into content decisions. Identifies winners and killers.

Weekly Responsibilities
  • Analyze CTR, retention, engagement for all content
  • Identify top 20% content patterns — replicate
  • Track lead attribution from content to CRM
  • Calculate revenue per content piece
  • A/B test thumbnail and title variants
Key Deliverables
  • Weekly performance report (dashboard)
  • Content ROI tracker (per piece)
  • Optimization action list (weekly)
