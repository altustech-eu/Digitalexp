Enterprise Intelligence
Market & Business
Intelligence Engine

The intelligence layer converts market data into decision velocity. It feeds every function — from campaign targeting to country expansion to pricing — with structured, actionable insights.

INTELLIGENCE 01
Market Research
Primary research (surveys, interviews), secondary research (government reports), field intelligence from counsellors. Output: Country opportunity reports, demand-supply gap matrix.
INTELLIGENCE 02
Marketing Research
Persona segmentation (UG, nurses, IT, parents), funnel analytics (Awareness→Conversion), campaign effectiveness. KPIs: CPL, CAC, ROI by persona.
INTELLIGENCE 03
Competitor Analysis
Map competitor offerings, pricing, claims, conversion flows. Identify positioning gaps. Output: Competitive gap map, counter-messaging playbook.
INTELLIGENCE 04
Web Research & Scraping
Auto-scrape job boards, career pages, government sites. Extract salary, demand, employer contacts. AI: NLP classification of job demand trends.
INTELLIGENCE 05
Labour Demand Forecasting
Country-wise shortage occupations (Healthcare, Ausbildung, IT, Logistics). Wage vs cost arbitrage analysis. Talent supply mapping by source country.
INTELLIGENCE 06
Social Listening
Track behavioral signals across platforms. Detect viral patterns, sentiment trends. Output: Real-time opportunity alerts, content angle recommendations.

Your real asset is not candidates or employers — it is structured intelligence converted into decision velocity. The organization that acts on market signals 48 hours faster than competitors wins the intake cycle.

FOCO Model

Franchise Owned, Company Operated — highest control, higher investment, best for priority markets like Germany and UAE.

Franchise Invested, Company Operated — shared investment, company-managed quality. Ideal for mid-tier expansion markets.

Joint venture with local player. Fastest entry, shared risk. Best for unfamiliar regulatory environments like Eastern Europe.

Stage 3
Counseling &
Conversion Engine
Counselor Role
Core Revenue Role
Responsibilities
  • Assess academic and financial eligibility of each candidate
  • Map candidate profile to optimal program (Ausbildung, Nursing, Study Abroad)
  • Explain program, timeline, cost, training requirement, and placement process
  • Handle objections with evidence — testimonials, employer letters, success data
  • Conduct parent counseling sessions for under-22 candidates
  • Close enrollment and initiate registration workflow
  • Maintain CRM hygiene — every interaction logged within 2 hours
Key KPIs
  • Lead-to-Counseling Rate: Target >50%
  • Counseling-to-Registration Rate: Target >30%
  • Response SLA Compliance: <2 hours from lead creation
  • CRM Update Compliance: 100% same-day update
Deliverables
  • Completed counseling session form (all fields)
  • Updated CRM stage and next action
  • Proposal / brochure sent to candidate
  • Parent counseling completed (where applicable)
